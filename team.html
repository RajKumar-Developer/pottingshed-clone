Our Team

Potting Shed was founded in 2007. We are an award-winning, full-service design, advertising & digital agency with offices in Guernsey and Jersey and a client base stretching to Europe, Asia, Australasia, and the Middle East. We work in all industry sectors from International brands to small retail outlets. We believe passionately in the power of well-crafted design solutions and in truly listening to our clients.
